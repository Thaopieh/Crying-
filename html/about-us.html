Leading online travel agency Trip.com has today announced that it will be partnering with three hotels namely The Salil Hotel Riverside - Bangkok, Chatrium Hotel Riverside Bangkok, and Holiday Inn Bangkok Sukhumvit to extend its sponsorship of CHUANG ASIA by offering special rooms at these hotels themed around the hit reality idol search programme. Starting from today, the public will be able to make bookings for these exclusive CHUANG ASIA themed rooms for the period of 15 March to 20 April 2024. The room rates will start from THB 5,600 per night at The Salil Hotel Riverside - Bangkok, THB 5,500 per night at Chatrium Hotel Riverside Bangkok, and THB 4,940 per night at Holiday Inn Bangkok Sukhumvit.

Featuring thoughtfully designed rooms full of CHUANG ASIA decorations and souvenirs, each room promises to be a treat for fans of the show. Some of the highlights include a random mentor poster, a random trainee poster autographed by the respective trainees, and a CHUANG ASIA windmill origami folding as seen on TV. Users who book these rooms also stand to win a TEAM WANG design trainee kit, a welcome pack that each CHUANG ASIA trainee received at the start of the programme, autographed mentor posters, and other limited edition CHUANG ASIA collectibles such as trainee-autographed Trip.com suitcases, by participating in activities on Trip.coms social media. Said Ms Diane Cui, General Manager, Trip.com Thailand: “As the presenting sponsor of CHUANG ASIA, Trip.com is delighted to see how popular it has been with audiences here in Thailand. The enthusiasm shown by fans in voting for their favourite trainees has been irresistible, and this has encouraged us to offer this special experience in exclusive CHUANG ASIA themed rooms at these popular hotels. At Trip.com, we always want to offer our users special experiences, and we hope they will enjoy their stays at these rooms just as much as they enjoy watching the trainees work towards being the next big thing in entertainment.”
